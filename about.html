About

This site is part of a Windsor.ai connector test harness for the Microsoft Clarity Data Export API.

How the data is generated

Visitors land on a page, the Clarity tag fires, and Clarity records the session in its dashboard. After Clarity's daily rollup, the /export-data/api/v1/project-live-insights endpoint surfaces aggregates broken down by Browser, Device, OS, Country/Region, Source, Medium, Campaign, Channel, and URL.

Things to try

  • Visit each page from a different browser.
  • Open the site on a phone and a desktop.
  • Append ?utm_source=test&utm_medium=manual to populate the Source / Medium dimensions.
  • Scroll to the bottom and click around.
